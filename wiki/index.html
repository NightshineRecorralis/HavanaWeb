Getting started

Welcome to HavanaMC.com! Havana is a town-building server set on planet Earth.


In Havana, your buildings and bases are protected by creating a town and claiming the land you construct in.


To view how to make a town, go to the section "Town system". Once ingame, you can type /help to view info.


You can quickly travel to the place you want to build by visiting the OUTPOST WARP NPC, which is just forward out of spawn (-4482, 63, -1365)


You can only use this NPC's warp one time, so make it worth it! Each item in the menu warps to a different region of the world.


Brazil: -3355, 67, 921
China: 7519, 65, -2624
Congo: 1532, 74, 301

Texas: -6095, 83, -2243

Antarctica: 2434, 123, 4509

There are seven outposts (about one on each continent). Each outpost sells and buys items through various merchants. All outposts have a unique merchant that sells items other outposts might not! They also all have an auctioneer where you can access global auctions.



Town system

To make a town, type /town create your_town_name_here. You must have at least $250, and your town name must match the location you are making it in!


Invite your friends to your town with /town invite your_friends_name_here


Claim land for your town with /town claim. Claiming costs money, and after 8 plots owned, the price becomes exponential!


To make claims, you will need money in your town bank. Type /town deposit money_amount_here to deposit the specified amount of money into your town's bank.


To view all towny help, type /t ?


Brewing

BREWING LIST SPREADSHEET

To begin brewing, you need to place a lit fire below a cauldron filled with water.


Place ingredients in the cauldron by right clicking the cauldron while holding the item. Depending on what you insert will change the brew.


Right click on the cauldron while holding a clock to view how long it has been boiling for.



Once the brew you are making has boiled for enough time, right click while holding empty bottles to receive the first part of your brew.



Some brews need to be distilled before they are aged. To distill, place a brewing stand. Inside, place glowstone dust at the top with your temporary brew bottles in the bottom slots.



After distilling or boiling, your brew needs to age to gain quality. Brews age when being placed in barrels. Make a barrel by placing 8 stairs in the orientation shown in the image. The stairs at the front are the same way in the back.



To complete the barrel, place a sign at the bottom right stair reading "Barrel" on the first line. If you built it right, you will see a message in chat stating, "Barrel created"



Put your brews inside the barrel and wait for them to age.



A large barrel can be created like so. A fence should be placed on the bottom middle plank, with a sign saying "Barrel" above it.

Wars

Havana includes a simple war system which allows for casual play and each war can last multiple real life days.



Attacking

You want to start a war? Ok.. You'll need to declare enemy status against the nation you want to attack. (For example, /n enemy add AtlanticFederation)
Now that you are enemies you are free to walk up to a member town of your enemy nation and place a non-white banner just outside of their claim.


Siege points

In order to win you will need to score siege points by occupying the wilderness area close to the siege banner, or killing enemy players in a large area around the siege banner.


Winning

You won? Congratulations! You can place a colored flag to invade this town and force it into your nation and you can also place a chest outside the town to plunder the town of some money.


Notes

Don't want to fight yet? Become peaceful with (/n toggle peaceful)
